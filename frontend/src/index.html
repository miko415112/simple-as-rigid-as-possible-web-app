Instructions

1. Add Models

- Click the button in the bottom-right corner to access preloaded .off models.
- Generate 2D Mesh by drawing: Click "Draw" to enable drawing mode. Select "Line" and draw lines (When you release the left mouse button, the endpoints will automatically connect). If the shape is wide, you can choose "Point" and add additional vertices in the middle and then press "Create" to create a 2D Mesh.
- Upload your own .off file (Note: This feature is not stable and may crash if there are too many triangles).

2. Add Control Points

- Click "Edit Constraint" and select "Add". Then, click on the desired control vertex on the model screen .
- Click "Apply Constraint" to make the constraint change from red to green, indicating it can be manipulated (this may take some time).

3. Move Control Points

- Click "Edit Constraint".
- Select "Move" to move the constraint. Moving the constraint will not immediately deform the shape. You can move multiple constraints and then click "Transform".
- Select "Move (instant transform)" to instantly transform the shape after moving the constraint.
